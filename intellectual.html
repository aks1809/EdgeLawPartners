Intellectual Property Rights

“It is easier to build and protect than to repair the broken property”

Edge Law Partners have been strong believer in protection of intellectual property and as an experienced Firm with strong background in engineering, biotechnology, designing as well as having professionals on board with strong intellect in the trademarks, copyrights, patents, designs and plant varieties, we take privacy very seriously and value every idea generated for the usage of clients’ intellectual property.

The Firm not only protects the rights contained therein for an intellectual property but assist and represent clients’ interests before various forums, registrars, high courts and Hon’ble Supreme Court for their usage and enforcement assuring that the IP is not infringed by any wrong doing of the others.

The team has covered large scale of matters both Pan India as well as international borders and have marked their spot as the ‘go-to’ Firm for clients IP needs in the sectors including automobiles, manufacturing, FMCG, software, technology, healthcare and pharmaceuticals, education, retail, media and broadcasting etc.

What keeps the Firm at Edge is the dedicated departments with proactive team members who are not only knowledgeable but tech savvy with digital age. With state of art infrastructure and software installed at the premises, we are able to provide the clients instant information on trademarks and patents and whether the client needs to entail into opposition / prosecution activities.
