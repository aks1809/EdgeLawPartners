Edge Law Partners is on the verge of becoming the fastest – growing energy law practice firm in the country. Working with clients across borders, the Firm has significantly handled matters related to oil and gas, power and electricity, and renewables and natural resources.

The team holds expertise in managing the risks and opportunities which come in the way of building and securing the energy related projects. The Firm has been at the forefront (having deeper understanding and insights of the laws) when representing its clients (banks, governments, regulators, contractors, suppliers, project developers, and stakeholders) on complex and diverse transactional matters.

Our diverse sectoral experience keeps us at Edge to perform our duties in the most intricate and complex matters. With increase in demand of energy along with increasing climatic changes, the Firm provides it’s clients assistance not only in the regulation and compliance but also with the policies interpretation and predictions.
