Real Estate

“The safest investment in the world managed with utmost care”

Edge Law Partners always have been on the brim when it comes to protection of the clients’ interests in the real estate matters and transactions. With the advent of Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, and the FDI regimes there has been significant increase in the demand of projects being registered and litigation being pursued.

The Firm has strived to provide cost – effective and efficient representation before various Tribunals, High Courts, and Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, putting logical and factual arguments with supportive evidences complying with local and central laws and regulations.

What keeps us at an Edge is the progressive relationships developed with the government authorities, contractors, developers, owners, landlords, tenants, borrowers, bondholders as well as gained multifarious expertise in terms of negotiations, approvals, investigations, research, risk assessment, and interpretation, providing just and equitable solution in complex situations.

Owing to commitment shown by the team members, the Firm has gained a strong clientele which includes High Net-worth Individuals (HNIs), Real Estate Developers Conglomerates, Multinationals and Private and Public Sector Units.

The Firm has played an eminent role in changing the face of the nation by handling matters related to smart cities, urban and rural townships, special economic zones, technology parks, hotels, shopping centres, and other residential and commercial real estate projects.
