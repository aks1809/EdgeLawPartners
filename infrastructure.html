Infrastructure & Project Finance

“Building assets that will make your growth sustainable”

Edge Law Partners over the years have helped set up multiple infrastructure products across the country and have been the few notable firms to collaborate and represent clients in foreign jurisdictions.

Whether it is the construction of a road, airport, port, railways, rapid transit system, and urban or rural infrastructure, the Firm has provided end-to-end solutions whilst advising banks and financial institutions investing / sponsoring the infrastructure projects.

The team has created a niche for themselves in the said practice and have represented regulators, government bodies, developers, investors, suppliers and contractors on designs, negotiations, operation, management along with regulation and compliance matters related therein.

What keeps us at an Edge is the focused and innovative approach applied towards every project with integrated and comprehensive advice of the Real Estate, Environment and Corporate Departments, providing the best possible and implementable solution to the client.
