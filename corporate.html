Corporate Law

“Matters derived directly from the life-cycle of a corporation”

Edge Law Partner’s indomitable experience and impregnable understanding of the Indian economy has kept its members at the pinnacle when clients seek advice for their business affairs. The sophistication coupled with sound knowledge of the field have made us a ‘preferred choice’ when clients look viable solutions.

Having gained popularity among the peers, the lawyers are well-versed with an organisation’s requirements and perform a significant role whilst advising clients on corporate legal advisory and transactional practice which covers planning, advising and executing day-to-day business legal and regulatory issues, governance, formation, funding, and winding up of a corporation.

The Firm brings with it an extensive and thorough understanding of issues relating to Companies Act, FEMA Rules & Regulations, SEBI & RBI guidelines, Foreign Direct Investments ("FDI") and compliances thereto, Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act (“FCRA” ) and all other related laws, regulations and guidelines issued by Government of India and regulatory authorities.

What keeps the Firm at Edge is the equitable approach and attitude adopted at every stage of the matter whether serving banks, multinationals, or governmental bodies with their transactional matters arising in automobile, automation, education, energy, financial services, FMCG, healthcare, insurance, infrastructure, manufacturing, media, real estate and technology sectors.
